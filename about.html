About

A LED light-up umbrella powered by a microcontroller and Arduino!

Made by Kathryn (Katy) Lee, this umbrella helps pedestrians during rainy days.

Different versions have been made, including one with a color sensor and another with an accelerometer.

Katy has presented and displayed her umbrella at Maker Faire Bay Area and Maker Faire New York.
